Cosmetic dentistry

A brighter smile, on your timeline

Professional teeth whitening lifts years of coffee and wine staining in about an hour, with take-home trays to keep it bright. It's our most-requested treatment and the most common reason new patients call.

For chips, gaps, and worn edges, porcelain veneers and composite bonding reshape a smile in a couple of visits. Ask about a free smile-makeover consultation with a digital preview.

Orthodontics

Straighter teeth, clear or classic

Invisalign clear aligners straighten teeth without metal — removable for meals and nearly invisible, with most cases finishing in 6 to 18 months. The first orthodontic consultation is free.

For teens and more complex bites, traditional and ceramic braces are still the most predictable option. We offer monthly payment plans on every orthodontic case.

Implants & restorative

Replace and repair, built to last

Dental implants replace a missing tooth root with a titanium post and a natural-looking crown — placed in-house, no referral needed. We quote the full treatment plan up front, with financing.

For damaged teeth, same-day crowns are milled and fitted in one visit, and root canal therapy saves a tooth that would otherwise be lost — comfortably, under local anaesthetic.
